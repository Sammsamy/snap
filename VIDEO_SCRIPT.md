## Wording boundaries

- Call biotin a small molecule, not a drug.
- Say candidate markers or candidate contacts, not confirmed hydrogen bonds.
- Say local score, not binding affinity.
- Call 1FN an experimental inhibitor, not an approved medicine.
- Do not claim a global optimum, predictive docking, clinical validation, or measured learning efficacy.
- Do not compare scores from different target-specific fields as affinity or binding strength.
